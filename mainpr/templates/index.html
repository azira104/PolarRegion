About the Website

Welcome to our online Polar Region data hub!

  • Explore the collection of information Provide all the public data collected from online data repository.
  • Data visualization Present some data visualizations that illustrate the relevant information.
  • Easy access to reliable data All information comes from scientific studies and observations.

This website is a collaborative effort with Yayasan Penyelidikan Antartika Sultan Mizan. This research project has involved a team of 6 dedicated members and collaborators working tirelessly to contribute their expertise. Together, we've pooled our resources, knowledge, and passion for the polar regions to compile a comprehensive database.
