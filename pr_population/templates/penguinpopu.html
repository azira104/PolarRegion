Penguin Population

The three sites are Neko Harbor, Petermann Island, and the Orne Islands, and the data included spans the period between 1984 and 2019.

Count data for three species of pygoscelid penguins at three sites near Anvers Island, Antarctica. The three sites are Neko Harbor, Petermann Island, and the Orne Islands, and the data included spans the period between 1984 and 2019. Data was accessed using the online Mapping Application for Penguin Populations and Projected Dynamics. Citation for MAPPPD: Humphries G.R.W., Che-Castaldo C., Naveen R., Schwaller M., McDowall P., Schrimpf M., and Lynch H.J. 2017. Mapping Application for Penguin Populations and Projected Dynamics (MAPPPD): Data and tools for dynamic management and decision support. Polar Records
